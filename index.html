About

AJILE is a Brooklyn-based trio exploring the space where blues, soul, and R&B meet improvisation.

Featuring guitarist and vocalist Aayushi Karnik, bassist Jaysin Glover, and drummer Layton Weedeman, the band creates original music rooted in groove, feel, and conversation.
