About

AJILE is a blues, soul, and R&B trio rooted in groove, feel, and improvisation. Featuring guitarist and vocalist Aayushi Karnik, bassist Jaysin Glover, and drummer Layton Weedeman, the band blends expressive songwriting, melodic bass lines, and deep-pocket rhythms into performances that leave room for spontaneity and conversation.
